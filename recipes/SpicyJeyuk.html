Spicy Korean Pork Stir Fry

Description

Spicy marinated pork bulgogi in a gochujang based sauce with a variety of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 150g of pork belly or other cut
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 carrot
  • ½ of a cabbage (nappa)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • ⅛ cup of water
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tbsp of sesame oil

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables & crush garlic
  2. Cut pork into bite/medium sized pieces
  3. Make sauce w soy, gochujang, garlic & water
  4. Add pork to pan on high heat
  5. Add green onions and sugar when pork is almost done
  6. Add the sauce before sugar starts to burn
  7. Add vegetables
  8. Mix and finish w sesame oil
