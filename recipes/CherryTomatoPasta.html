Cherry Tomato Pasta

Description

Incredibly simple, sweet, savoury and flavourful pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 450 g of pasta (Bowtie is best)
  • 3-5 Large tomatoes OR 750g of cherry tomatoes
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • Â¼ cup of virgin/extra virgin olive oil
  • ~12 frozen shrimp
  • Pinch of salt
  • Dried basil
  • Pepper
  • Chili flakes

Steps

  1. Thinly slice garlic
  2. Cut tomatoes into wedges ONLY IF using large tomatoes
  3. Defrost shrimp/seafood
  4. Boil and salt water for pasta
  5. Add pasta right before starting to cook
  6. Heat up pan to medium heat
  7. Add sliced garlic to pan for ~3mins (see a lil bit of browning)
  8. Add tomatoes (DON'T break cherry tomatoes)
  9. Wait till it breaks down to sauce
  10. Add defrosted shrimp
  11. Let shrimp cook and season w salt and pepper
  12. Add basil and chili flakes
  13. Put pasta into sauce
  14. Mix and add pasta water if needed
