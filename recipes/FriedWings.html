Air Fried Chicken Wings

Description

Juicy and crispy chicken wings that are quick to make and are incredibly delicious. Seasoned with salt, pepper and a special spice blend, you can add sauces to further enhance the taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 tbsp of garlic salt
  • 1 tbsp of spice blend
  • 1 tbsp of lemon pepper

Steps

  1. Pat dry the chicken wings and place in mixing bowl
  2. Coat with olive oil, garlic salt, spice blend, lemon pepper and toss to evenly coat with seasonings
  3. Place chicken in air fryer and air fry at 400F for 9 minutes per side or until crisp
